### Conditional Independence  
**Independent** ≠ **Uncorrelated**

e.g. When \( X \in [-1, 1] \), then \( Y = x^2 \). This can be expressed as:

\[ \text{Cov}(X, Y) = 0 \]

indicating that \( X \) and \( Y \) are **uncorrelated**, even though \( Y \) is completely determined by \( X \).
